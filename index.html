About

Hello, I'm Lilybeth Shields-Cruz welcome to my personal site. Professional learner and geek. I dedicate my life to the pursuit of knowledge, which is a really nice way of saying I am a student. Besides the study of teaching and language, I spend my time learning about creative writing, editing, photography and, most recently, some light programming. When I am not off honing my skills I play games, watch programs and engage in fan discussions with friends.
